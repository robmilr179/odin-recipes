Delicious and easy mashed potatoes!

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives

Directions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes
  2. Boil the potatoes
  3. Warm the milk and melt the butter
  4. Drain the potatoes
  5. Mash the potatoes
  6. Season to taste
  7. Serve with a side of your favorite meat!
